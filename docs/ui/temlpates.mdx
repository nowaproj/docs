Templates help you start faster by giving you ready-made screen designs that you can customize to fit your app.  
You can connect them to your logic, change colors, and apply your own branding — saving time while keeping your design consistent.

Nowa provides a variety of templates, including dashboards, music players, article pages, and more.

---

## How to Access Templates

To add a template to your project:

1. Select the **Screen tool** from the **Toolbar**.  
2. Scroll through the available templates and view their previews.  
3. Click the screen template you want to use.  
4. A popup will appear showing the files included in the template — choose which ones to import.  
5. Click **Import** to add the template to your project.
